# TrumpyTracker Product Vision

**Last Updated:** 2025-11-25  
**Owner:** Josh

---

## Core Value Proposition

**"Win the Argument Arsenal"**

When you're in a conversation and need receipts on Trump's BS, quickly find and share bullet points of all the dumb shit he's done. TrumpyTracker isn't competing with news sites - it's a **searchable accountability database with attitude**.

---

## Target User

Progressive/liberal audience who:
- Gets into political arguments and needs quick facts
- Wants curated "bullshit receipts" with attitude
- Values spicy, opinionated summaries over neutral reporting
- Needs to find specific topics/actors fast during conversations

**User Job:** "I'm arguing with someone about Trump and need to quickly pull up what he actually did."

---

## Product Pillars

### 1. Spicy Voice
- Progressive perspective - we don't "both sides" corruption
- Angry but truthful accountability journalism
- Profanity allowed when it lands
- Never bland corporate-speak

### 2. Searchability
- Find specific topics/actors fast
- Filter by category, severity, date
- Optimized for "receipt finding" use case

### 3. Shareability
- Easy to share stories in arguments
- Social sharing to X and Facebook
- Future: Quotable lines for copy/paste (TTRC-293)

### 4. Accountability Tracking
- Chronological record of actions
- Executive Orders analysis
- Story clustering from multiple sources
- Future: Timeline view of "dumb shit tracker"

---

## What We're NOT

- **Neutral news aggregator** - We have a clear progressive perspective
- **Breaking news source** - We aggregate and enrich, not break news
- **Social platform** - No comments, no user accounts (for now)
- **Both-sides journalism** - When Republicans are doing the damage, we say so

---

## Roadmap Direction

*Note: These are directional ideas, not scheduled features. No timelines.*

### Near-term Enhancements
- **Quotable lines** (TTRC-293) - Punchy one-liners for easy sharing
- **Better search UX** - Optimize for "receipt finding"
- **Permalink sharing** - Deep links to individual stories

### Future Features
- **Timeline view** - Chronological "dumb shit tracker"
- **Wins/Progress tracking** - When bad actors face consequences
- **Email newsletter** - Bring people back with updates
- **Action prominence** - Surface "What You Can Do" more visibly
- **Merch store** - Revenue stream + brand building

### Long-term Vision
- Comprehensive Trump accountability database
- Expand to track broader Republican agenda
- Community features for organizing
- Mobile app for quick reference

## Brand Voice Guidelines

### DO:
- Call out bullshit directly
- Name names
- Use dark humor and sarcasm
- Make it personal ("YOUR taxes," "YOUR rights")
- Vary framing: fascism, corruption, grift, cronyism, power grab

### DON'T:
- Be neutral or "balanced"
- Use clich√© openings ("In a shocking move...", "Once again...")
- Be cheesy with humor
- Make shit up
- Soften the truth

### Banned Openings:
- "This is outrageous..."
- "In a shocking move..."
- "Once again..."
- "It's no surprise..."
- "Make no mistake..."
- "Let that sink in..."
- "Guess what?"
